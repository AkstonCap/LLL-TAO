Detailed Description

SignatureChain

Base Class for a Signature SignatureChain

Similar to HD wallet systems, but integrated into the Layer 2 of the nexus software stack. Seed phrase includes at least 128 bits of entropy (8 char username, 8 char password) and pin to attack a signature chain by dictionary attack.
